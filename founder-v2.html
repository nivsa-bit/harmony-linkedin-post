We just launched Harmony. And I want to tell you what made us build it.

Every week, I talk to sales leaders who say the same thing:

"We bought an AI call center. It was great for a month. Then it stopped getting better. Now we spend more time maintaining it than it saves us."

That's the dirty secret of this industry. Most AI call centers are frozen in time. You deploy them, they work at a fixed level, and the only way to improve them is to rewrite prompts, hire a specialist, or wait for the next vendor "optimization cycle."

Meanwhile, your best human SDR learns something new on every call. They share it with the team at standup. The whole team gets better. That feedback loop — learn, share, align — is what makes great sales teams great.

No AI call center has that. Until now.

We built Harmony around three beliefs:

1. Agents should learn from each other.
When one agent discovers that a specific objection handling doubles close rates — every agent should know it by the next call. Not next month. Not after a retune. Automatically. Same techniques, same best practices, same product knowledge. One team brain.

2. You shouldn't need a prompt engineer to run an AI team.
Setup should take hours, not weeks. No scripting. No prompt templates. Connect your CRM, your call recordings, your knowledge base — and Harmony builds agents from what already works for you. A simple UI that any sales leader can use, not a developer console that needs an engineering ticket.

3. Trust should be earned before the first real call.
The reason people don't trust AI agents is that they've been burned. Agents go off-script. They sound robotic. They handle the easy calls and choke on edge cases. So we built simulation-first training: run your agents through hundreds of synthetic conversations against real customer personas before they ever pick up a live phone. You see exactly where they fail, fix it, and go live with confidence.

And because we own our voice engine end-to-end, you're not paying hidden markups or dealing with third-party latency. Your agents sound natural, respond fast, and you control every aspect of the experience.

Harmony is headless — it connects to your CRM, your tools, your LLM. It doesn't replace your stack. It makes it smarter.

If you've switched AI call center vendors and still aren't satisfied — the problem isn't the vendor. It's that nobody built an AI team that actually learns, actually shares, and actually gets better without you having to babysit it.

That's what we built. Link in comments.
