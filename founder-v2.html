We just launched Harmony. Here's why we built it.

Every week I hear the same thing from sales leaders: "Our AI call center was great for a month. Then it stopped getting better. Now we spend more time maintaining it than it saves us."

Your best human SDR learns on every call and shares it at standup. The whole team improves. That feedback loop is what makes great teams great. No AI call center had that. Until now.

Harmony is built on three beliefs:

1. Agents should learn from each other.
One agent discovers a better objection handling? Every agent knows it by the next call. Same techniques, same product knowledge, one team brain. Automatically.

2. No prompt engineer required.
Connect your CRM and call recordings. Harmony builds agents from what already works for you. Simple UI any sales leader can use — not a developer console.

3. Trust is earned before the first real call.
Train agents against hundreds of synthetic customer personas first. See exactly where they fail, fix it, go live with confidence.

We own our voice engine (no hidden costs, sub-100ms latency). Harmony is headless — it connects to your CRM, your tools, your LLM. It makes your stack smarter.

If you've switched AI call center vendors and still aren't satisfied — the problem is that nobody built an AI team that actually learns, shares, and aligns without babysitting.

That's what we built. Link in comments.
