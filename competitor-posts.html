Key difference: this was posted from the company page, not a founder's personal profile. Also, the "1 million knowledge workers" goal feels abstract. Relevance AI's LinkedIn presence is significantly weaker than ElevenLabs — this is an opportunity for Harmony.

Patterns & Takeaways for Harmony

Founder Posts Crush Company Posts

ElevenLabs' founder posts get 15K+ likes vs 1-4K on company page posts. The #1 and #2 posts are both from Mati's personal profile. Your launch post should come from you personally, not just the OneAI page.

Video Drives Dwell Time

All top 5 posts include video. LinkedIn's 2026 "Depth Score" algorithm rewards seconds spent consuming content. The 8:46 origin story video (#2) outperformed many shorter posts. Long-form video + story arc = algorithm fuel.

Concrete Numbers Win

"$330M ARR", "$11B valuation", "35K+ calls", "zero to 350 people" — every high-performing post leads with specific, impressive numbers. Avoid vague claims. Show receipts.

Relevance AI Is Weak on LinkedIn

Their best post has 200 likes from 40K followers (0.5% engagement). ElevenLabs gets 5-15K from 291K (2-5%). Relevance AI's LinkedIn presence is not a threat. This is whitespace for Harmony to own.

The Playbook for Harmony's Launch Post

Based on these patterns: Post from your personal profile, not the company page. Include a short video (1-2 min). Lead with the pain, not the product. Drop 2-3 concrete metrics or customer results. Tell a story arc (past pain → insight → what you built → vision). End with a question or invitation to drive comments (15+ word comments are weighted 15x more than likes). Post on Tuesday-Thursday between 8-10am your audience's timezone.
